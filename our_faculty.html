Our Principal

Dr. Sudha Venkatappa V – Principal & Chairman

Dr. Sudha Venkatappa V is a visionary leader and dedicated educator who has been at the helm of Nayana Vidyanikethana Patashale as its Principal and Chairman for the past six years. With a rich experience of over 15 years in the field of education, she has played a pivotal role in shaping the institution’s academic excellence and holistic development.

Her leadership is driven by a passion for fostering a nurturing and dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to think critically, innovate, and excel. Under her guidance, the school has witnessed significant advancements in curriculum development, student engagement, and extracurricular initiatives.

Dr. Sudha firmly believes that education extends beyond textbooks and classrooms. She is committed to empowering both students and teachers, ensuring that learning remains an evolving and enriching experience. Through her progressive vision and unwavering dedication, she continues to inspire the next generation of learners and educators alike.

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it is about shaping character, instilling values, and preparing individuals to contribute meaningfully to society."
