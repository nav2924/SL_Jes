Admission Process 2024-25

1. Application Process

April 1 - April 30

Begin your journey with us by submitting the application form:

  • Collect application form from school office
  • Submit completed form with required documents
  • Attach 2 passport-size photographs
  • Submit previous academic records

2. Written Assessment

May 15 - May 30

All applicants will undergo age-appropriate assessments:

  • Grade 1-5: Basic aptitude test
  • Grade 6-8: Subject-specific written test
  • Grade 9-10: Comprehensive entrance exam
Note: Admit cards will be issued 1 week prior to test date

3. Parent Discussion

June 1 - June 15

Final step in the admission process:

  • Meeting with principal and faculty members
  • Discussion about school philosophy and values
  • Q&A session about curriculum and activities
  • Final admission decision communication

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: April 30, 2024
  • Test Date Announcement: May 5, 2024
  • Merit List Publication: June 20, 2024
  • Academic Year Begins: July 1, 2024
